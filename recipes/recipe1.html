What's In Zuppa Toscana?

These are ingredients you'll need to make this hearty zuppa toscana recipe:

  1. Meat:This meaty zuppa toscana soup calls for both mild Italian sausage and bacon.
  2. Spices:Crushed red pepper flakes and fresh garlic lend bold, spicy flavor.
  3. Vegetables:You'll need an onion, six potatoes, and fresh spinach.
  4. Broth:Use store-bought or homemade chicken broth.
  5. Cream:A cup of heavy cream is essential for this rich, decadent soup.

How to Make Zuppa Toscana

You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here's a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade zuppa toscana:

  1. Cook the Italian sausage with the red pepper flakes.
  2. Cook the bacon, then cook the onion and garlic in the bacon drippings.
  3. Add the broth and bring to a boil, then add the potatoes and simmer.
  4. educe the heat and stir in the cream, cooked sausage, and spinach.

How to Store Zuppa Toscana
